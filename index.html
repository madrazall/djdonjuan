The Man Behind the Music

He doesn't just play music —
he feels the room.

DJ Don Juan has been doing this for over two decades, and it shows. He cut his teeth in the nightclub circuit — reading crowds, dropping beats that kept dance floors packed until last call — and that instinct for energy never left him.

These days, he brings that same magic to your wedding day and special events. He knows when to bring the heat, when to slow it down for a first dance that makes the whole room go quiet, and exactly when to drop that song that gets grandma and your college friends on the floor at the same time.

Based in Central, CT — serving all of Connecticut and beyond.
